In 2003, I built a pseudo social network and was nearly kicked out of high-school.

Since then, I’ve been rebelliously crafting my skill as a design director for digital and interactive platforms.

By day, I'm an Associate Design Director for Estee Lauder Online, working with Bumble and bumble. At Bumble I lead UX/UI design strategy and execution of digital creative while managing a team of designers + front-end engineers.

When I’m not at my desk I’m most likely hanging with my pup, Norman or wandering the streets of LA, shooting photos for my street style blog, fthis.nyc.
